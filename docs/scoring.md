# Scoring

**TODO**: The scoring of the participants' solutions will be based on a combination of metrics that reflect the success and efficiency of the cable insertion task. The exact scoring formula will be defined, but it is expected to include the following:

*   **Success Rate**: Whether the cable was successfully inserted into the correct port. This will be the primary metric.
*   **Insertion Time**: The time taken to complete the insertion task, from the start signal to the successful insertion.
*   **Precision**: The accuracy of the insertion, e.g., how well-aligned the cable is with the port.
*   **Contact Forces**: The forces and torques exerted during the insertion process. Lower forces will be rewarded to encourage gentle and safe manipulations.
*   **Penalties**: Penalties may be applied for collisions with the environment, exceeding force limits, or other undesirable behaviors.
